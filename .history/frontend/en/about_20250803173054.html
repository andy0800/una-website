About Distinguished Union Institute for Private Training

Who We Are

The Distinguished Union Institute for Private Training is a leading educational institution in Kuwait, licensed by the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training, and accredited by the Civil Service Commission. We provide professional training programs in international arbitration, cybersecurity, and administrative development.

Our Vision

We strive to be the first choice for those seeking to develop their professional skills, through training programs that keep pace with technical, legal, and administrative developments worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality training that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring that trainees acquire professional experience that can be applied directly in the work environment.

Our Activities

  • Private training institute licensed by the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training
  • Accredited by the Civil Service Commission of Kuwait
  • Authorized and accredited by AUPS Digital University
  • Providing professional training programs in international arbitration, cybersecurity, and administrative development
